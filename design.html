How to Use AI Dock Design Studio

Follow these simple steps to get dock design ideas from our AI.

1

Enter Dock Description

In the "Describe your ideal dock" box, enter a detailed description of your desired dock. The more detailed you are, the better the AI can generate ideas close to your wishes. For example: material type, size, shape, purpose of use, accompanying amenities (roof, seating, lights,...), or design style.

2

Click "Generate Idea" Button

After entering the description, click the "Generate Design Idea" button. The AI system will analyze your request and provide a detailed conceptual sketch for you.

3

View Results & Explore More

The results will be displayed below, including concept name, vision, features, suggested materials, and style. You can then click on the "✨ Maintenance Tips ✨" or "✨ Accessory Ideas ✨" buttons to get more useful information related to the newly generated design.

4

Clear and Try Again (If Needed)

If you want to try a different idea, click the "Clear Input" button to empty the description box and start over. You can experiment with different descriptions to find the idea that suits you best.

Note: AI Design Studio provides initial suggestions. For detailed design and accurate quotes, please contact KhaDock directly.
