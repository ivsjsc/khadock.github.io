Boat Dock Design Thinking Process

  1. Site Analysis: Assess the waterfront, water depth, and environmental factors to determine the best dock layout.
  2. Client Vision: Collaborate with the client to understand their needs, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences.
  3. Concept Development: Sketch initial ideas, considering function, safety, and integration with the landscape.
  4. Material Selection: Choose durable, weather-resistant materials suitable for marine environments.
  5. Visualization: Create 3D renderings and plans to help the client visualize the final dock design.
  6. Refinement & Approval: Adjust the design based on feedback and finalize for construction.
