Project: Mr. Jimmy's Dock in Saint Petersburg

A full renovation project, upgrading an old wooden dock into a modern and safe outdoor living space.

Hurricane-Tested Durability: This dock was remodeled with composite materials in Clearwater, FL. After two major hurricanes in 2025, it remains intact and fully functional. Strong docks, peace of mind.
