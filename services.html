What We Offer

From initial concept to completion and maintenance, Kha Boat Dock is with you every step of the way.

Design

Custom & Creative Dock Design

Our design team works closely with you to create a dock that not only meets functional requirements but also reflects your personal style. We consider every factor:

  • Site analysis, hydrological conditions, and environment.
  • Optimization of space and functionality.
  • Selection of suitable, durable, and aesthetic materials.
  • Intuitive 3D designs to help you visualize the structure clearly.
  • Compliance with Florida's safety regulations and standards.
Request Design Consultation
Construction

Precise & Durable Dock Construction

With a team of skilled craftsmen and professional project management, Kha Boat Dock ensures your project is built to the highest quality, on time, and within budget.

  • Use of premium materials: treated wood, composite, marine-grade aluminum.
  • Advanced construction techniques ensuring accuracy and safety.
  • Close supervision of all construction phases.
  • Adherence to Florida's building and environmental standards.
  • Meticulous finishing for beauty and long-lasting durability.
Start Your Construction Project
Installation

Modern Boat Lift Installation

Protect your prized boat and optimize dock space with a high-quality boat lift system. We supply and install:

  • V-Hull Lifts (Bottom Standing Lifts).
  • Overhead Lifts / Suspended Lifts.
  • Personal Watercraft (PWC) Lifts.
  • Custom solutions for various boat types and weights.
  • Professional installation ensuring safety and smooth operation.
Learn About Boat Lifts
Maintenance

Routine Dock Repair & Maintenance

To keep your dock in top condition and extend its lifespan, Kha Boat Dock offers comprehensive repair and maintenance services:

  • Periodic inspections and dock condition assessments.
  • Structural repairs, replacement of damaged decking and pilings.
  • Maintenance of electrical and water systems at the dock.
  • Cleaning, removal of algae, barnacles.
  • Upgrading and renovating existing docks.

Renovation

Dock Remodeling & Upgrades

If your current dock no longer meets your needs or you desire a fresh look, Kha Boat Dock's remodeling service is the perfect solution. We help you:

  • Assess current conditions and advise on optimal renovation solutions.
  • Expand, change the structure or layout of the dock.
  • Upgrade materials, add new amenities (seating, lighting, roofing).
  • Modernize the dock, increasing aesthetic appeal and value.
  • Transform an old dock into a new, convenient, and upscale space.
