Design

Custom & Creative Dock Design

Our design team works closely with you to create a dock that not only meets functional requirements but also reflects your personal style. We consider every factor:

  • Site analysis, hydrological conditions, and environment.
  • Optimization of space and functionality.
  • Selection of suitable, durable, and aesthetic materials.
  • Intuitive 3D designs to help you visualize the structure clearly.
  • Compliance with Florida's safety regulations and standards.

For more details, please contact us directly.

Renovation

Dock Remodeling & Upgrades

If your current dock no longer meets your needs or you desire a fresh look, KhaDock's remodeling service is the perfect solution. We help you:

  • Assess current conditions and advise on optimal renovation solutions.
  • Expand, change the structure or layout of the dock.
  • Upgrade materials, add new amenities (seating, lighting, roofing).
  • Transform an old dock into a new, convenient, and upscale space.

For more details, please contact us directly.

Maintenance

Routine Dock Repair & Maintenance

To keep your dock in top condition and extend its lifespan, KhaDock offers comprehensive repair and maintenance services:

  • Periodic inspections and dock condition assessments.
  • Structural repairs, replacement of damaged decking and pilings.
  • Maintenance of electrical and water systems at the dock.
  • Cleaning, removal of algae, barnacles.
  • Upgrading and renovating existing docks.

For more details, please contact us directly.
